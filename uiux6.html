POS System

---

Role: Web UI/UX Designer Year: 2025
Figma Client Collaboration Application Design

The Brief & Challenge

This commissioned freelance project was for a billiard venue that also operated a bar and restaurant. The client wanted a unified POS system that could handle different needs: booking billiard tables, managing bar and restaurant orders, and processing payments seamlessly.

The key challenge was that unlike a standard POS, this system needed to integrate a QR-based booking flow so customers could scan, book, and pay directly from their phones. At the same time, the bar and restaurant POS had to remain simple and efficient for staff to operate. Another deliverable was a digital booklet that explained the system features, making onboarding and client presentation easier.

This project highlighted the importance of translating diverse business operations into a single, intuitive experience for both customers and staff.

Process & Outcome

I started by mapping out the different user journeys: customers booking and paying for billiard tables via QR, staff managing ongoing sessions and bar orders, and admins overseeing reports and inventory. Wireframes and prototypes were created in Figma, iterating closely with the client to refine each flow.

The final design combined three business models—billiard, bar, and restaurant—into one seamless POS system. Alongside the interface, I delivered a comprehensive digital booklet to guide onboarding and stakeholder presentations. The solution reduced confusion between services while giving customers a fast, frictionless experience.
