Case Study

Notora, Smarter Note-Taking App

Transforming notes from simple storage into active learning material with intelligent, automated features.

Role: UI/UX Designer Year: 2025
Figma User Research Mobile Design

Context & Problem

Traditional note-taking apps only provide storage, they don’t actively support learning or knowledge retention. Users often struggle with information overload and forget to revisit important points.

Passive

Note Storage

Low

Knowledge Retention

Research & Insights

User interviews with students revealed two key pain points: taking notes during fast-paced lectures is stressful, and reviewing long notes before exams is time-consuming. This insight highlighted an opportunity for automation.

"How can we turn passive notes into active study aids automatically?"

The Solution: An Intelligent Study Flow

We designed Notora around the typical study flow, integrating AI-powered features at each critical step.

STT Integration

Real-time transcription for lectures or meetings.

AI Summarizer

Note condensation to highlight key points.

Flashcard Maker

Auto-generated flashcards to support active recall.

Final Designs & Outcome

The result is Notora, a productivity app that evolves beyond traditional note-taking. The "summarize + flashcard" combo was highly valued in feedback for reducing study prep time.
