Case Study

Narraitive, Health Tracking with Humanized Insights

Making health data accessible and empowering for users who struggle with raw metrics.

Role: UI/UX Designer Year: 2025
Figma User Research Mobile Design

Context & Problem

While useful, health tracking metrics can be overwhelming, especially for elderly users. Important health signals are often ignored simply because users cannot translate complex numbers into meaning.

Data

Overload

Lack of

Meaning

Research & Insights

Interviews with older adults and caregivers revealed a desire for clear, empathetic feedback rather than charts alone. This confirmed the need for a system that not only tracks, but also “tells the story” of the user’s health.

"Users don't want more charts; they want to know if they're doing okay."

The Solution: A Health Companion & Guide

We envisioned NarrAItive as a companion that translates data into stories and actionable advice.

Narative Reports

Automatic daily summaries written in natural language.

Simple Recommendations

Actionable tips tied directly to the user's data trends.

Health AI Assistant

A conversational AI to answer health questions in plain language.

Final Designs & Outcome

The prototype focused on accessibility with large fonts and voice narration. Feedback showed appreciation for “health storytelling” as it reduced anxiety around complex numbers.
