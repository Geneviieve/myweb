Grevur, Circular Economy App (UI/UX Case Study)

Role: UI/UX Designer Year: 2025
Figma User Research Mobile Design

Co-designed Grevur, a mobile app concept that promotes sustainable living through secondhand trading and waste deposit rewards. The team developed a prototype that allows users to exchange used goods or deposit recyclable waste in return for redeemable points. Integrated AI features such as a carbon impact estimator and an eco-assistant chatbot to enhance user engagement and promote informed sustainability choices.
