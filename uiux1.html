Case Study

Grevur, Circular Economy App

Merging secondhand trading with waste desposit rewards to make sustainable living simple and rewarding.

Role: UI/UX Designer Year: 2025
Figma User Research Mobile Design

Context & Problem

In Indonesia, waste sorting habits remain low, while secondhand trading is fragmented. People want to live more sustainably, but the process often feels inconvenient and unrewarding.

Low

Waste Sorting Habits

Fragmented

Secondhand Market

Research & Insights

Through desk research and user interviews, we found a key insight: people are willing to trade used items but see no direct benefit in sorting waste. This led us to a core idea:

"What if we connect these two behaviors with a clear, unified incentive?"

The Solution: Three Core Pillars

We designed Grevur to be a friendly, transparent, and motivating app built on three pillars to encourage sustainable habits.

Goods Exchange

A secondhand marketplace for trading items.

Waste2Points

A waste deposit system with redeemable points.

Eco-AI Assistant

An AI chatbot and impact estimator to guide users.

Final Designs & Outcome

Using Figma, we created high-fidelity prototypes that received positive feedback, confirming our design direction of "one app for both selling and recycling."
